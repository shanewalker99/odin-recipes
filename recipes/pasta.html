Pasta

Handmade pasta, perfect to accompany any sauce of choice!

Nothing beats some handmade homemade pasta, fresh in taste and perfect in bite

A little more effort than dried pasta, but you'll thank me later after experiencing FRESH!

Ingredients:

  • Flour (All-Purpose) - 16 oz, more for kneading/rolling pasta dough.
  • Egg Yolks - 16 each
  • Olive Oil - 1 Tbsp
  • Salt - 1 1/2 Tsp

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in food processor. Blend until a rough ball forms
  2. Remove from food processor to a lightly floured work surface
  3. Knead until dough springs back to the touch, and is smooth but not sticky
    • Note that if its sticking to your surface, use a slight amount of flour to dust the ball
  4. Cover the dough and let rest for 10-15 minutes to fully hydrate the flours and decrease elastisity
  5. Sheet through a pasta roller, or roll by hand to very thin layers
  6. Shape/cut to your favorite pasta shapes (Ravioli/Agnolotti are personal favorites with this dough)
  7. Enjoy!
