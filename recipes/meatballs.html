Meatballs

Hand-rolled meatballs, perfect to accompany some pasta!

Juicy little morsels of meat, hand kneaded and rolled, delicious on their own or amazing covered in your sauce of choice

This recipe is perfect for any night, as it is quick and easy, requires only a few ingredients, and tastes DELICIOUS!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef (80/20) - 2 Lbs
  • Breadcrumbs - 1/2 Cup
  • Eggs - 2 Each
  • Parmesan (Grated) - 1/2 Cup
  • Italian Seasoning - 2 Tbsp
  • Salt - 1 Tbsp
  • Black Pepper (Ground) - 1 1/2 Tsp

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients
  2. Mix well by hand. The meat should look emulsified with all ingredients, not crumbly
  3. Take a small portion (mini-meatball sized) and cook fully in a pan. Check for salt/seasoning
  4. Adjust salt/seasoning to your preference
  5. Form into small meatballs by hand, size is your preference as well
  6. To cook, either sear and finish in a sauce of choice, or bake on a sprayed sheet-tray at 375 degrees F
  7. If unsure if meatballs are cooked, their internal temperature should be above 145 degrees F at least
